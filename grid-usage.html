Desktop to Mobile

Don't want your columns to simply stack in smaller devices? Use the extra small and medium device grid classes by adding .col-xxs-* .col-xs-* .col-sm-* .col-md-* .col-lg-* to your columns. See the example below for a better idea of how it all works.

.col-lg-8 .col-md-8 .col-sm-6 .col-xs-6 .col-xxs-12

.col-lg-4 .col-md-4 .col-sm-6 .col-xs-6 .col-xxs-12

Offsetting columns

Move columns to the right using .col-md-offset-* classes. These classes increase the left margin of a column by * columns.
For example, .col-md-offset-4 moves .col-md-4 over four columns.

**Note: You can also override offsets from lower grid tiers with .col-*-offset-0 classes.**

.col-md-4

.col-md-4 .col-md-offset-4

.col-md-3 .col-md-offset-3

.col-md-3 .col-md-offset-3

.col-md-6 .col-md-offset-3

HTML 
									
<div class="row text-center">

	</div>

	<!-- content here -->
	</div>
</div>

	<div class="col-md-3 col-md-offset-3">
		<!-- content here -->
	</div>
	<div class="col-md-3 col-md-offset-3">
		<!-- content here -->
	</div>
</div>
<div class="row text-center">
	<div class="col-md-6 col-md-offset-3">
		<!-- content here -->
	</div>
</div>

Nesting columns

To nest your content with the default grid, add a new .row and set of .col-sm-* columns within an existing .col-sm-* column. Nested rows should include a set of columns that add up to 12 or fewer (it is not required that you use all 12 available columns).

Level 1:
.col-sm-9

Level 2:
.col-xs-8 .col-sm-6

Level 2:
.col-xs-4 .col-sm-6

HTML 
									
<div class="row">
	<div class="col-sm-9">
		<!-- content here -->
		<div class="row">
			<div class="col-xs-8 col-sm-6">
				<!-- content here -->
			</div>
			<div class="col-xs-4 col-sm-6">
				<!-- content here -->
			</div>
		</div>
	</div>
</div>

Column ordering

Easily change the order of our built-in grid columns with .col-md-push-* and .col-md-pull-* modifier classes.

.col-md-9 .col-md-push-3

.col-md-3 .col-md-pull-9

HTML 
									
<div class="row">
  <div class="col-md-9 col-md-push-3">
  	<!-- content here -->
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-3 col-md-pull-9">
  	<!-- content here -->
  </div>
</div>
